The IIIT Contact Directory is a comprehensive database designed to store student and faculty information. Our objective is to provide an extensive and intuitive platform that simplifies the search for contact details and other relevant information.

~/About Page: $~ Future enhancements

While the current version of the IIIT Contact Directory is functional and serves its purpose, we have plans for future enhancements. Some potential improvements include:
